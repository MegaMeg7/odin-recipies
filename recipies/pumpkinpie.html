Pumpkin Pie

Description

How to make a pumpkinpie from scratch-yum!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cups of pumpkin puree
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 1cup heavy cream
  • whipped cream for topping

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Create pie crust by mixing flour, butter, and cream cheese together.
  3. Wrap in plastic wrap and set aside in fridge
  4. Cut pumpkin in half and carefully remove seeds. Optional: save seeds for later roasting!
  5. Place pumpkin halves cut side down onto baking sheet. Bake for 45 minutes
  6. After cooling, remove skin.
  7. Cut pumpkin into manageable chunks and blend/food process.
  8. Turn oven up to 425 degrees.
  9. Roll out your pie crust. Tip: Coat rolling surface in flour to prevent sticking.
  10. Place crust in pie pan, removing excess by pinching and folding the top.
  11. Stir togethre sugar and spices in mixing bowl. In a separate container, mix pumpkin puree, maple syrup, vanilla, eggs heavy cream, and your spice mixture
  12. Pourmixture into pie crusted pan.
  13. Bake for 15 minutes, then turn oven down to 350 degrees and bake until the pie is set (about 90 minutes)
  14. Let cool, and serve with whipped ccream, ice cream, or no cream.
