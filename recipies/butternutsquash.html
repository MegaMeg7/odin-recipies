Roasted Butternut Squash with Walnuts and Goat Cheese

Description

An easy side or main dish featuring warm and sweet-savory roasted Butternut Squash.

Ingredients

  • 1 butternut squash
  • 1 package of goat cheese
  • 1 package walnuts
  • 1 stick butter
  • 4 tablespoonsmaple syrup
  • Salt and peper to taste

Steps

  1. Prehead toven to 375 degrees.
  2. Peel squash, cut in half, and remove seeds.
  3. Thinly slice butternut squash and place in a grease 9x12 baking dish or pan
  4. Toss Squash in melted butter, salt, and pepper.
  5. Roast until soft (Tip: test with fork) about 30-45 minutes.
  6. Oncecsoft, remove from oven and toss squash in maple syrup or sweetener of your choice.
  7. Top with crumbled goat cheese and chopped walnuts
  8. Enjoy!
