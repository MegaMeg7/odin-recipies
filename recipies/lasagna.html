Lasagna MegaMeg7's Style

Description

My favorite way to make lasagna. This recipie has combinedmy favorite aspects of lasagna from trial and error, and trusted sources to create my favorite version to date.

Ingredients

  • Homemade tomato sauce
    • <1i>One package of oven-ready lasagna noodles
  • 1 32 oz container of cottage cheese
  • 1 regular sized bag/container of shredded parmesan
  • 1 package of shredded cheddar or colby jack cheese
  • 1 lb of italian pork sausage
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Brown Italian sausage in a skillet. Drain grease and set aside.
  3. mix cottage cheese, parmesan, and italian seasoning together in a mixing bowl. Add seasoning to taste.
  4. Layer the following in a 9x12 glass baking dish: tomato sauce, lasagna noodles, cheese mixture, italian sausage. You should be able to fit about 2 layers. End your last layer with tomato sauce.
  5. Top lasagna with a final layer of shreded cheddar or colby jack cheese
  6. Cover baking dish with aluminum foil.
  7. Bake for 45 minutes to an hour. Optional: Remove foil for last 15 minutes if a crispy texture is desired.
  8. Let lasagna cool for at least 15 minutes
  9. Top with basil to taste, and enjoy!
